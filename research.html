This is the most common challenge new immigrants who come from non-English
countries face in Canada. Language barriershave become a hurdle that new
immigrants must overcome to integrate themselves into Canada.

Research Goals

Our research aims to delve into the specific challenges new immigrants face with language barriers. We want to identify its concrete impact and help new immigrants identify and enhance existing language support systems. From there we want to provide profound insights and effective solutions to improve the quality of life for new immigrants and foster social integration.

Research Core Questions

What are some types of language barriers?
What impact does language barriers have on new immigrants’ employment in Canada?
How does language barriers influence the social interaction of new immigrants?
What potential mental health issues do language barriers pose for newcomers?
What language training support does Canada provide for new immigrants?
What impact does language barriers have on new immigrants education?

Problem Statements
