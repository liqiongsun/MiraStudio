Kalmont Mountain Ski Resort Website

Project Description

Through the Kalmont project, I designed and developed a responsive skiing website to enhance the client’s online presence. The project covers the entire process, from research and planning to design and final implementation using HTML/CSS , ensuring seamless functionality across all devices.

Challenge

The initial design went smoothly, but during coding, I struggled to implement the ticket purchasing section due to my limited HTML and CSS skills as a beginner. The sitemap outlines the structure of the skiing website, showing the main pages and their hierarchical relationships.
