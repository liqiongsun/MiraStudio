Welcome to PixelMuse Virtual Gallery

Project Description

This project combines the concept of a virtual art gallery with a commercial shop, creating a platform that showcases global artworks while supporting art sales. By leveraging technologies like VR and AR, it breaks geographical barriers, making art more accessible.

The platform not only promotes emerging artists by providing visibility but also connects them with buyers, offering a seamless space for both appreciation and commerce, enhancing the value of art in the digital age.
