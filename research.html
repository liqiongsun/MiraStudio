Research on language barriers for new immigrants integrating into Canada

Ideation and Design Thinking l Brand Design l UI Design

Project Information

This project aims to explore the specific challenges that new immigrants face in terms of language barriers, study the impact on employment, social interaction, education, and mental health, and provide design solutions to improve language support systems.

Process

This project used different tools, like personas, wireframes, and storytelling, to explore how a language app could help new immigrants overcome language barriers. The app adapts to each user's level, offering personalized lessons that make learning easier and more relevant. In the end, the project focused on creating a customized solution that helps new immigrants improve their communication skills, making it easier for them to adjust to life in a new country.
