SkiSilk Brand System

Project Discription

Skisilk is a skincare brand I developed, drawing on five years of experience in brand strategy within the cosmetics industry. I bring expertise in every stage of brand development, from product concept and design to market launch and promotion, ensuring a cohesive customer experience. While I excel in design, messaging, and market positioning, I acknowledge the complexities of manufacturing that may require external expertise. Skisilk embodies my passion for combining strategic thinking with creative execution, delivering a brand that truly resonates with its audience.
